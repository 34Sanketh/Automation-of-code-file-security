The PRD Method

The single most important prompting framework. Vague in = vague out.

P
Problem
What exactly are you solving? Be specific. "I need to land interviews at fintech startups for a PM role"
R
Role
What expert should AI be? "You are a hiring manager at Razorpay who has reviewed 500+ resumes"
D
Deliverable
What output do you want? "Give 5 specific changes with before/after examples for each"
